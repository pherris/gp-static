Bianchi. Innovation through the decades

Bianchi, the world's oldest bicycle manufacturing company and the brand we’ve carried the longest. We love Bianchi so much that we decided to paint our store celeste, the color of Bianchi. Come by and check out the storefront along with the wide variety of Bianchi bikes we carry.

Wilier Triestina. See for yourself.

Wilier Triestina has been a family run bike manufacturer since 1906. Wilier bikes truly embrace Italian flair and ride like a dream. But don’t take our word for it, come by and ride one so you can experience why we love them so much.

Pinarello. a tour de france champion.

Pinarello’s sole purpose is to create the greatest race bike on this planet and it is hard to argue with their list of victories. Their unique style and panache brings something everyone can appreciate.

Colnago. the heritage shines through

Colnago might not be as old as Bianchi or Wilier, but when they still make bikes in Mr. Colnago’s basement. There is no shortage of history to go around.

MOOTS. the last weld in Titanium

MOOTS bikes are the best made titanium bikes hands down. If you would like to argue that, then come by and take a look at these works of art.

KONA. Ride with your own style

KONA is bicycle brand based out of the pacific northwest and specialize in mountain and cross bikes. These guys just love to develop bikes that are fun to ride and you see it across their whole line. From bombing downhill to commuting, KONA develops a bike that makes riding a blast.

Ritchey. Old-school attitude, new-world performance.

Ritchey is a legendary bike builder from the USA and designs cutting edge steel frame bicycles. Tom Ritchey stills designs and tests product out of his garage in California and who wouldn't want to buy a bike from a man that looks this awesome.
